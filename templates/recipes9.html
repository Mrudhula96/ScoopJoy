Mexican Hot Chocolate Ice Cream Sandwiches

Ingredients

  • Milk, cream, sugar
  • corn syrup
  • cocoa processed with alkal
  • Natural Flavor
  • guar gum, cellulose gel

Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 325F. Combine flour, cocoa, salt, cayenne, cinnamon, and baking powder. Set aside.

2. Mix butter with brown sugar and beat on medium high until light and fluffy (about 3 minutes). Beat in egg and vanilla until well mixed. Reduce speed to low and add dry mixture. Stir in chocolate.

3. Scoop 2 ounce balls of dough, flatten slightly, and coat with turbinado sugar. Bake for 9-11 minutes or until surface cracks slightly. Allow to cool.

4. Slice the pint of Dulce de Leche horizontally, remove cardboard, and place the round discs between cooled cookies.

5. Roll the outside of the sandwiches in the crushed cinnamon candy if desired.
