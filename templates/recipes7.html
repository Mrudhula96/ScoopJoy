Lemon Frose

Ingredients

Instructions

1. Using a high speed blender, add 3 Outshine® Lemon Frozen Fruit Bars, frozen strawberries and 1/4 cup of water as needed. Blend until smooth.

2. Pour into a short glass.

3. Top with a lemon slice and a fresh Outshine® Lemon Frozen Fruit Bar.
