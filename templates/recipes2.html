Peppermint Bark Ice Cream Float

Ingredients

  • 2 cups fresh sliced strawberries
  • Whipped cream
  • 1/2 cup of butter
  • 1/3 cup of sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoons almond extract

Instructions

1. In a medium mixing bowl, combine cocoa powder, sugar, chocolate, and salt.

2. In a medium-sized saucepan on medium heat, bring milk to a near scalding boil. Pour milk immediately over the bowl of cocoa, sugar, chocolate and salt. Allow it to sit for 1 minute..

3. Whisk slowly to combine. Continue whisking until mixture is evenly combined and silky smooth. You can pass it through a fine mesh strainer if there are lumps.

Pour hot chocolate evenly into mugs, scoop Häagen-Dazs® Peppermint Bark Ice Cream on top.

Optional: garnish with a candy cane or peppermint stick... it's fun!
