Raspberry Chocolate Dipped Bars

Ingredients

  • raspberries (fresh or freeze-dried)
  • dark or milk chocolate
  • cocoa butter
  • milkfat
  • emulsifiers like soy lecithin

Instructions

1. In a large microwave safe bowl, melt chocolate chips and coconut oil in the microwave. Heat at 30 second intervals until softened.

2. Dip each Outshine® Raspberry Frozen Fruit Bar in the mixture and coat all sides, the place on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.

3. Sprinkle freeze dried raspberries, chopped pistachios, Valentine’s Day sprinkles, or any toppings of your choice on one side of the bar.

4. Once all are complete, transfer to the freezer.

5. Once the bars are set, enjoy!
