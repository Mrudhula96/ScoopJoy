Mango Tajin Frozen mocktail

Ingredients

  • mango (fresh or frozen)
  • ice, Tajin seasoning
  • lime juice
  • honey or maple syrup
  • orange juice or seltzer.

Instructions

1. Using a high speed blender, add ice, Outshine® Mango with Tajín® Frozen Fruit Bar and lime juice. Blend until smooth.

2. Moisten the rim of a glass with a lime wedge. Dip the rim of the glass into the Tajín® Clásico Seasoning and twist to coat.

3. Pour the frozen mocktail into the glass; serve with a fresh Outshine® Mango with Tajín® Frozen Fruit Bar, a slice of lime and enjoy.
